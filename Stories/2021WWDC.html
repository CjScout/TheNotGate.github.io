Everything Important Apple Announced at 2021 WWDC

6-8-2021

By Connor

Apple announced a lot on Monday, here's all the important stuff.

IOS 15

Facetime Revamp

Shareplay

Apple's new shareplay feature allows for movies, music, podcast and many other things to be shared with other's while on a FaceTime call. You can even share your screen!

New Audio & Video Options

Apple has introduced Portait & Mic modes. You can now blur your background and remove background noise. They also introduced a new grid view, similar to Zoom.

FACETIME LINKS

You can now schedule zoom calls using links. Using these, anyone can now join a FaceTime call. Including people on Windows & Android. I'm personally super excited for this one.

Shared With You & Collections

When someone shares something with you, it will automatically be put in a new shared with you section in the corisponding app. Large number's of images are also stacked or collaged together.

Focus & Notifications

Do not disurb now has modes, when enabled, you can choose what notification you get. Notificatons also stacks similar notification into a summary for easy access.

Maps & Safari

Maps now has more detailed instructions, including AR instrctions. Safari can now install extentions, and a new tab bar design.

Wallet, Inteligence, & Privacy

You can now add ID's to your wallet, and new text recognition in images. Spotlight now has increased information, and all audio recognition for siri never leaves the device. You can also see what data apps are accessing and what services they are connecting to.

Various New Widgets

iPadOS 15

Widgets & Homescreen

Widgets will be able to be placed anywhere on the home screen, and some apps will have a new larger size widget to take advantage of the iPads screen space. The App Library is also coming to iPad.

Multitasking

A new multitasking men can be pulled down from the top. When a option is selected, the current app will move to the side, and reveal the homscreen. When a second app is selected, both apps will appear in the format you selected in the multitasking menu.

A new shelf allows for having multiple copies of an app to be open at the same time, and the app switcher allows for you to drag two apps on top of each other to create split view windows.

Notes

By swiping up from a corner, you can now create a quick note, as well as organize using tags and notify using mentions

Swift Playgrounds & Universal Control

You can now develop and publish apps for iPhone & iPad using the swiftUI library. Apple also informed us about Universal Control, a new way to control your iPad using your mac.

MacOS Monterey

Streamlined Safari

A new tab bar design takes up less space and a more powerful search field. They also introducted tab groups to keep track of things.

Shortcuts & Universal Control

Shortcuts are now coming to macOS! Poweruser's, this is your update. As perviously mentioned, macOS and iPadOS are getting Universal Control, which allows for controlling your iPad with your Mac and easier file transfers.
